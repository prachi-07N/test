Bondi Beach

Bondi Beach is one of Australia’s most famous beaches, located 7 km east of Sydney’s central business district. Known for golden sand, surfing, and the iconic Bondi Icebergs pool, it attracts locals and tourists alike.

Visitors can swim, surf, or take the scenic Bondi to Coogee coastal walk. Cafes and shops along the promenade make it a lively area year-round.
