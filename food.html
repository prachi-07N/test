Culture of Sydney

Sydney is a multicultural and vibrant city known for its outdoor lifestyle, cafés, art festivals, and friendly people. The city encourages healthy eating, and vegetarian options are widely available in almost every restaurant. Sydney also celebrates many cultural events such as Vivid Sydney, New Year fireworks, and food festivals throughout the year.
