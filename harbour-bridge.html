Sydney Harbour Bridge

The Sydney Harbour Bridge is a steel arch bridge connecting the Sydney central business district (CBD) and the North Shore. Known locally as “The Coathanger”, it is one of Sydney’s most recognized landmarks.

Visitors can climb the bridge, walk or cycle across it, or enjoy stunning views from the nearby lookout points. It’s an essential part of Sydney’s skyline and a must-visit attraction.
